Economic Trends and Poverty Rates in the United States Using machine learning to predict poverty

This project provides a statistic portrait of the effect of various economic factors on poverty rates in the United States over the past four decades. The poverty measures examined were minimum wage, unemployment rate, welfare and education spending, homeownership, crime rate, and divorce rate at both the state and national levels.
